Business Overview

Broadcom Inc. is a premier technology company innovating and marketing semiconductor and infrastructure software solutions worldwide. Operating through Semiconductor Solutions (approximately 55% of revenue post-VMware) and Infrastructure Software (approximately 45% of revenue), Broadcom is positioned at the nexus of technology evolution.

Thesis Details

1 Dominance in Semiconductor Solutions

With a robust portfolio in networking, broadband, and storage, the semiconductor segment is poised for accelerated growth, capturing the market shift towards AI and IoT applications.

2 Strategic VMware Acquisition

The $61 billion VMware acquisition is a game changer, catapulting Broadcom into a leadership position in the hybrid cloud and virtualization software industries, merging superior hardware and software capabilities.

3 Exceptional Capital Allocation

Known for its disciplined capital management, Broadcom consistently returns value to shareholders through strategic buybacks and a dividend yield of 3.5%.
