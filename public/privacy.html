Privacy Policy

Initiate Coverage iOS App

Last updated: February 2026

Introduction

Initiate Coverage ("we," "our," or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our iOS application and website at initiatecoverage.com.

By using Initiate Coverage, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

Information We Collect

Account Information

  • Email address and Google account information (name, email, profile identifier) when you sign in using email or Google Sign-In
  • Account credentials are used solely for authentication and syncing your data across devices

Watchlist and Portfolio Data

  • Stock tickers you add to your watchlists and portfolios
  • Watchlist names and organization preferences
  • This data is stored to provide personalized portfolio tracking across your devices

Search and Query Data

  • Stock tickers you enter to generate research reports or look up market data
  • Search queries are used to retrieve relevant financial data and generate AI-powered analysis

Usage Data

  • Pages visited and features used within the app
  • General interaction patterns to help us improve the app experience
  • Device type and operating system version for compatibility purposes

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and maintain the app's core functionality, including personalized watchlists and portfolio tracking
  • Generate AI-powered stock research reports and market analysis tailored to the tickers you request
  • Sync your watchlists and preferences across devices when you are signed in
  • Improve the app experience, fix bugs, and develop new features
  • Retrieve real-time market data, news, and financial information relevant to your interests

Third-Party Services

Initiate Coverage relies on the following third-party services to deliver its functionality. When you use the app, certain data may be shared with these providers as described below:

OpenAI

We use OpenAI's API to generate AI-powered research reports, market analysis, and stock explanations. When you request a report or analysis, the relevant stock ticker(s) and query context are sent to OpenAI's servers for processing. OpenAI's usage policies and privacy practices apply to data processed through their service.

Exa AI

We use Exa AI for intelligent news search. Stock tickers and related search queries may be sent to Exa's servers to retrieve relevant news articles and information.

Yahoo Finance

We retrieve real-time and historical stock prices, company financials, and market data from Yahoo Finance. Stock tickers you search or add to your watchlist are sent to Yahoo Finance to fetch the corresponding data.

CNN

We retrieve Fear & Greed Index data from CNN to display market sentiment information. No personal user data is shared with CNN.

Google Sign-In

If you choose to sign in with Google, we use Google's OAuth 2.0 authentication service. We receive your name, email address, and a unique account identifier from Google. We do not access your Google contacts, calendar, or any other Google services beyond authentication.

Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored on secure servers. We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

When you are signed in, your watchlists and portfolio data are synced across your devices through our servers. If you use the app without signing in, your data is stored locally on your device.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. Your data is shared with third-party services only as necessary to provide the app's core functionality, as described in the "Third-Party Services" section above.

We may disclose your information if required to do so by law or in response to valid legal requests by public authorities.

Data Retention and Deletion

Your account data, including watchlists and portfolio information, is retained for as long as your account remains active. Cached research reports may be retained for up to 30 days to improve performance and reduce redundant processing.

You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us at privacy@initiatecoverage.com. Upon receiving a verified deletion request, we will remove your personal data from our servers within 30 days.

Children's Privacy

Initiate Coverage is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have inadvertently collected such information, we will take steps to delete it promptly. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, please contact us at privacy@initiatecoverage.com.

Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Object to or restrict certain processing of your data
  • Request a portable copy of your data

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at privacy@initiatecoverage.com.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we make changes, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Continued use of the app after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

Financial Disclaimer

Nothing in this app constitutes investment advice, financial advice, trading advice, or a recommendation to buy, sell, or hold any security. AI-generated content may contain errors or inaccuracies. Always conduct your own research and consult a licensed financial advisor before making any investment decisions. Past performance is not indicative of future results.
