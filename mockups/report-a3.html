Business Overview

Broadcom Inc. is a global technology leader that designs, develops, and supplies semiconductor and infrastructure software solutions. The company operates through two segments: Semiconductor Solutions and Infrastructure Software.

Founded in 1961 as a division of Hewlett-Packard, the company has grown through strategic acquisitions including Brocade, CA Technologies, and most recently VMware in a landmark $69 billion deal that closed in November 2023.

Broadcom's semiconductor business generates 50%+ gross margins with minimal capex requirements, resulting in industry-leading free cash flow conversion.

Thesis Details

01 AI Networking Leadership

Broadcom's custom AI accelerators (XPUs) and networking solutions position the company at the center of hyperscaler AI infrastructure buildouts. The company has design wins with three of the four major cloud providers.

02 VMware Integration

The VMware acquisition provides Broadcom with a recurring revenue software business that complements its semiconductor operations. Management targets $8.5B in run-rate synergies within three years.

Key Risks

Customer concentration remains elevated with Apple representing approximately 20% of semiconductor revenue. Integration execution risk on VMware. Cyclical exposure to enterprise IT spending.
